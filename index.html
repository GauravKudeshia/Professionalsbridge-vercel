Hard Lessons From Bad Applications

Most application problems are fixable once they are visible.

★☆☆☆☆

Generic resume

One resume used everywhere rarely tells the strongest story for a specific job.

★☆☆☆☆

Missing keywords

Good experience can still be missed when the language does not match the role.

★☆☆☆☆

Weak interview prep

Most candidates prepare generic answers instead of practicing from their own resume.

★☆☆☆☆

Unclear impact

Bullets without metrics, scope, or outcomes make strong work look ordinary.

★☆☆☆☆

Formatting risk

Complex resume layouts can create parsing issues before a person ever reads them.

★☆☆☆☆

No job match view

Without a match report, it is hard to know why a recruiter should choose you.
